About The Clever Accounants

The Clever Accountants, a subsidiary of Empreneur Group Corp, has been in business since 2014. The very first client, which was the start of newborn success, was a national non-profit organization.

Gustavo worked hours to ensure his business ran successfully by balancing every business account down to the penny. At the same time, his company continued to grow as a brand.

He can currently provide his accounting services and proactively help your business grow and plan for the next move. The Clever Accountants is a well-qualified and trained team dedicated to helping you relieve financial stress and giving you spare time to focus on your business.

Founder's Story

The Clever Accountants founder, Gustavo Velazquez, at the age of 12, arrived in the United States from Mexico in summer 1998.

A year after high school, he worked at Bank of America as a personal banker. He loved hearing customers talk about their businesses. Despite his youth, he advised and coached his business owner customers. As a result, his business owner customers earned their confidence and trust in him. He realized that being an entrepreneur was his passion.

He always felt like he wanted to do things differently from everyone else. In 2008, at the age of 22, he started a logistics company. In his first year, he faced many obstacles to establish his business, including the unexpected economic crisis.
