About The Clever Accounants
The Clever Accountants, previously known as Empreneur Group Corp, has been in business since 2016. The very first client was a national non-profit organization that started his snowball of success. Gustavo worked hours to ensure the business ran successfully by balancing every business account down to the penny while growing his company as a brand. Founder's Story

After high school, he worked at a bank and loved hearing customers talk about their businesses, which inspired him to start his own.
